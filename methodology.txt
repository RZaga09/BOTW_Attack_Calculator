Order of Operations
1. Base Damage
2. Attack Modifier
3. Attack Up
4. Bonus vs X Enemy
5. Ancient Proficiency/Bone Attack Up
6. Critical Hit/Sneakstrike/Shatter
7. Elemental Damage

Attack Up:
	Level 1 +20%
	Level 2 +30%
	Level 3 +50%

Bonus vs X Enemy:
	Guardian Weapon vs Guardian +30%
	Ancient Weapon vs Guardian + 50%

Ancient Proficiency/Bone Attack Up:
	+80% if Guardian/Ancient Weapon with Ancient Proficiency
	+80% if Bone Weapon with Bone Attack Up

Critical Hit/Sneakstrike/Shatter:
	Critical Hit x2
	Sneakstrike x 8
	Shatter x3
	Precedence Order: Shatter -> Sneakstrike -> Critical Hit
	Sneakstrike and Shatter Ovverride Elemental Damage

Elemental Damage:
	Fire + 10
	Ice + 10
	Electric + 20
	Bomb Arrow + 50
	Electrical in Rain + 40
	Enemy Immune to Elemental - no elemental damage
	Enemy Under same Element - no elemental damage
	Enemy under different Element - elemental damage applies

*Master Sword always 60 base damage against Guardians/Blights/in Hyrule Castle (NOT IMPLEMENTED)

*Edge Cases for MultiShot Bows (NOT IMPLEMENTED)
	Attack Modifier Applied per Arrow
	Fire Arrow +10 for only one arrow
	Shock Arrow +10 for only one arrow
	Ice Arrow + 10 for arrow 1 and x3 for arrow 2
	Shock Arrow in Rain + 40 for all arrows
	Bomb Arrow + 24 per arrow
	Bomb Arrow + 24 only for 2 arrows if 3-shot or 5-shot
